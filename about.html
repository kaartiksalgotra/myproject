We are here to help you experience the best of Jammu.

Welcome to Jammu Journeys, a dedicated student project created to showcase the breathtaking beauty, vibrant heritage, and rich culinary traditions of Jammu. Often referred to as the 'City of Temples', Jammu is the winter capital of the Indian union territory of Jammu and Kashmir.

Our mission is to provide an aesthetic, accessible, and comprehensive guide for travelers and explorers looking to discover the hidden gems of this majestic region. Whether you are seeking spiritual peace, scenic mountain views, or a taste of authentic Dogra cuisine, we have carefully curated the best experiences for you.

This website is built entirely from scratch using fundamental web technologies—HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript—ensuring a clean, human-crafted experience. Thank you for visiting!
