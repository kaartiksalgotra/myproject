Famous Food of Jammu

Experience the rich and authentic flavors of Dogri cuisine.

Main Course

Rajma Chawal

Rajma Chawal is one of the most famous dishes of Jammu, prepared with red kidney beans cooked in rich spices and served with steamed rice. The dish is loved for its authentic Dogri flavor and comforting taste.

Street Food

Kaladi Kulcha

Kaladi Kulcha is a popular street food made using traditional Dogra cheese called Kaladi. The cheese is fried until crispy outside and soft inside, then served in a bun with chutneys and spices.

Traditional Thali

Dogri Dham

Dogri Dham is a traditional festive meal served during weddings and celebrations in Jammu. It includes dishes like rajma, dal, ambal, khatta meat, and rice served in an authentic style.

Main Course

Khatta Meat

Khatta Meat is an authentic Dogra dish prepared with mutton cooked in a tangy gravy made using pomegranate seeds and spices. It is famous for its unique sour and spicy flavor.

Side Dish

Ambal

Ambal is a sweet and sour pumpkin dish cooked with jaggery, tamarind, and spices. It is a traditional part of Dogri meals and festive food in Jammu.

Condiment

Anardana Chutney

A tangy, spicy traditional chutney made from crushed dried pomegranate seeds, mint, and green chilies. It perfectly complements every Dogri meal.

Patisa

Patisa is a delicious, flaky sweet similar to Soan Papdi, famously made in Jammu. It is rich in ghee and melts in your mouth.

Kud Ka Patisa

A specialized version of Patisa that originates from the town of Kud, located on the Jammu-Srinagar highway. It is a famous delicacy highly sought after by travelers.

Babru

Auriya
