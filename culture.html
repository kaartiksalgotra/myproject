Culture & Heritage

Art

Basohli Paintings

Basohli Paintings are a famous form of Pahari miniature art known for their vibrant colors, bold expressions, and detailed designs. This traditional art style represents the rich artistic heritage of Jammu.

Dogra Traditions

Dogra Culture reflects the bravery, hospitality, and traditions of the Dogra community. The culture is known for traditional attire, folk music, dance, and strong cultural values.

Festival

Lohri Celebration

Lohri is celebrated with great joy in Jammu through bonfires, traditional songs, dances, and sweets. The festival marks the end of winter and brings families together in celebration.

Language

Dogri Language

Dogri Language is the traditional language spoken by the people of Jammu and is recognized as one of India’s official languages. It represents the cultural identity and heritage of the Dogra community.
